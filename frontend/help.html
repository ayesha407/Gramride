Need Help? 🤝

We are here to support you in booking your rides quickly and easily. Whether you're a farmer, a worker, or just traveling between villages — GramRide is your travel buddy.

  • 📞 Call our Helpline: 1800-123-GRAM (4726)
  • 🏠 Visit Nearby GramRide Kiosk: Find help at village panchayat centers
  • 🎤 Use Voice: You can speak your pickup/drop in your local language
  • 📍 Use GPS: Tap 📍 to auto-detect your current location
  • 🚜 Choose Vehicle: Based on distance and need – Bike, Car, Auto, or Tractor
  • 📄 Need Help with Booking? Ask any nearby volunteer or kiosk assistant

We're always here to help you ride safely 🚗💨
